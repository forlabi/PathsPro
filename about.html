About PathsPro

PathsPro is a web application that helps microwave and fiber planning engineers determine the Microwave Line of Sight (LOS) distance and fiber route distance to the closest Points of Presence (POP) from which a customer's location can be serviced.

Our goal is to provide accurate and efficient planning tools for engineers to optimize microwave and fiber network deployments.
